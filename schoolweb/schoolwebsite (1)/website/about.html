About Elite Victory School

Our History

Founded in 1990, Elite Victory School has a rich history of providing quality education to students in our community. Over the years, we have grown from a small institution to a well-respected school known for academic excellence and a commitment to student development.

Our Values

At Elite Victory School, we believe in:

  • Integrity: Upholding honesty and strong moral principles.
  • Respect: Valuing diversity and treating everyone with dignity.
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in education.
  • Community: Building a supportive and collaborative environment.

Programs Offered

We offer a variety of educational programs designed to meet the needs of all students:

  • Early Childhood Education
  • Elementary School
  • Middle School
  • High School
  • Special Education Services
  • Extracurricular Activities (Sports, Arts, Clubs)

Our Facilities

Elite Victory School School is equipped with modern facilities to enhance the learning experience:

  • State-of-the-art classrooms with smart boards
  • Science and computer labs
  • Library with a vast collection of resources
  • Sports facilities including a gymnasium and playing fields
  • Art and music rooms for creative expression

Community Involvement

We believe in the importance of community engagement. Our school actively participates in local events and encourages students to volunteer and give back to the community.
